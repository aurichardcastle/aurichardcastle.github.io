Presupposed vs Asserted Falsehoods

Presupposition framing roughly doubles false-premise acceptance in frontier LLMs (24.7% vs 12.2%, p < .001). Tested GPT-4o, Claude Sonnet 4.5, and Gemini 2.0 Flash across 1,793 responses using a blinded LLM judge with cross-validation.

Sichuan Dialect: Perception and Textual Absence

40-song corpus study showing Chengdu hip-hop's perceived Sichuan dialect is near-absent from written lyrics. Chinese orthography can't encode pronunciation; the dialect lives in performance, not text. Mixed-methods design with AI-assisted coding and native speaker verification.
