Presupposed vs Asserted Falsehoods

2026

I noticed AI kept agreeing with me. I wanted to know if the way you phrase a false claim changes whether a model catches it.

  • Within-subjects experiment across GPT-4o, Claude Sonnet 4.5, and Gemini 2.0 Flash
  • Full pipeline built in Python: stimulus generation, multi-threaded API collection via OpenRouter, blinded LLM-as-judge scoring with independent cross-validation
  • Study 1 hit a ceiling effect on well-known myths. Diagnosed the problem, redesigned stimuli, Study 2 found the effect

Current AI safety benchmarks systematically undercount sycophantic failures by missing the most natural way people phrase false claims.

24.7% vs 12.2% p < .001 1,793 responses

Sichuan Dialect: Perception and Textual Absence

2025 - 2026

I heard Sichuan dialect everywhere in Chengdu's hip-hop clubs. Then I opened the lyrics and it wasn't there.

  • 40-song corpus across 9 artists, 2,400+ lines coded for dialect features with native speaker verification
  • 4 of 5 Chengdu artists had zero confirmed dialect in their written lyrics. Comparison group also zero
  • One artist (KKECHO) deliberately forced dialect vocabulary into text, validating the method

Chinese characters encode meaning, not pronunciation. Any corpus-based study of dialect in Chinese music will systematically miss what listeners actually hear.

Jane Street Neural Network Reassembly

2026

Jane Street broke a trained neural network into 97 pieces and posted it on Hugging Face. We decided to put it back together.

  • Search space: (48!)^2, roughly 10122 possible configurations
  • Discovered that training leaves a dynamic isometry fingerprint in weight products, collapsing the pairing problem
  • Hill-climbed 66 adjacent swaps to converge from MSE = 0.076 to floating-point-zero error across 6 rounds
10122 search space MSE = 7.8 × 10-15 66 swaps
