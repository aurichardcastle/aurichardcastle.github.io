Presupposed vs Asserted Falsehoods

Does syntactic framing change whether LLMs accept false premises? Presupposition framing roughly doubles false-premise acceptance in GPT-4o, Claude Sonnet 4.5, and Gemini 2.0 Flash. Within-subjects experimental design with blinded scoring and independent cross-validation.

24.7% vs 12.2% p < .001 1,793 responses

Sichuan Dialect: Perception and Textual Absence

Listeners insist Sichuan dialect is central to Chengdu hip-hop. A 40-song corpus shows it's near-absent from written lyrics. Chinese characters encode meaning, not pronunciation, so the dialect exists in performance but disappears on the page. Mixed-methods design with fieldwork in Chengdu.

40 songs 2,400+ lines coded 9 artists

Jane Street Neural Network Reassembly

Reassembled a shuffled 48-block residual network from a 10122 search space. Training leaves a dynamic isometry fingerprint in weight products that collapses the pairing problem. Hill-climbed 66 adjacent swaps to floating-point-zero error.
